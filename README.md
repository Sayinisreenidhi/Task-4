📊 Task 4: Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on transforming raw business data into meaningful insights using the Sample Superstore dataset. It combines data storytelling with statistical validation to support data-driven decision-making. By analyzing sales, profit, discounts, and regional performance, the project identifies business trends and provides actionable recommendations.
🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales and profit performance.
Identify key business trends and patterns.
Create a data-driven business story using visualizations.
Validate insights using statistical hypothesis testing.
Provide actionable business recommendations.

📈 Key Insights
Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.
Profitability varied across different regions.
Higher discounts negatively impacted overall profit.
Some products achieved high sales but delivered lower profit margins.
Regional performance analysis highlighted opportunities for business improvement.
📊 Statistical Validation
Hypothesis
H₀ (Null Hypothesis): Discount has no significant relationship with Profit.
H₁ (Alternative Hypothesis): Discount has a significant relationship with Profit.
The Pearson Correlation Test was used to evaluate the relationship between Discount and Profit. The statistical analysis helped validate whether discount strategies significantly affect profitability.
💡 Business Recommendations
Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability.
Focus on high-performing product categories.
Strengthen sales efforts in underperforming regions.
Monitor both sales growth and profit margins.
Use data-driven insights for strategic business decisions.
